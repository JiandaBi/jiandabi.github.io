Dr. Jianda Bi is an Assistant Professor at the Business School of Zhejiang Wanli University. He received his Ph.D. in Finance from Nottingham University Business School China and previously worked as a Research Assistant at the Centre for Inclusive Finance, University of Nottingham Ningbo China. His research focuses on financial technology, innovation networks, text-based machine learning, large language models, and policy evaluation, with particular attention to how digital technologies shape firm innovation, technological progress, and industrial organization. Methodologically, his work combines production network theory, machine learning, and large-scale patent text data to study technological relatedness, innovation activities, and knowledge diffusion across firms. His research has been published in <em>Frontiers in Public Health</em>, <em>China Economic Quarterly</em>, and <em>China Journal of Econometrics</em>, and his working papers have been presented at AMES, CES China Annual Conference, and NASM.

To contact me, please email <a href="mailto:jianda.bi@zwu.edu.cn">jianda.bi@zwu.edu.cn</a>.

毕坚达，博士，现任浙江万里学院商学院讲师，博士毕业于宁波诺丁汉大学商学院金融学专业，曾任宁波诺丁汉大学普惠金融中心助理研究员。主要研究方向包括金融科技、创新网络分析、文本机器学习、大语言模型与政策评估，重点关注数字技术、企业创新、技术进步与产业组织变迁等问题。研究中主要结合生产网络理论、机器学习方法和大规模专利文本数据，分析企业技术关联、创新活动与知识扩散机制。相关成果发表于 <em>Frontiers in Public Health</em>、《经济学（季刊）》和《计量经济学报》等期刊，并曾在 Asian Meeting of the Econometric Society（AMES）、Chinese Economists Society China Annual Conference（CES China Annual Conference）、North American Summer Meeting of the Econometric Society（NASM）等国际会议报告工作论文。
